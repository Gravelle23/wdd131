Southern-MD-Crabbing.com - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Site Name: Southern-MD-Crabbing.com
This name is chosen to represent a site dedicated to crabbing in the Southern Maryland region. It's location-specific, ensuring the target audience can easily find information related to crabbing in this area.
Optional domain availability: southern-md-crabbing.com

Site Purpose

The website will provide crabbing enthusiasts in Southern Maryland with information on the best crabbing spots, tips for a successful crabbing experience, seasonal schedules, equipment recommendations, and local regulations. The site will also feature a community forum for crabbing enthusiasts to share experiences and tips.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

Where are the best crabbing locations in Southern Maryland?

Scenario 2:

What kind of equipment do I need for crabbing in the Chesapeake Bay?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2C3E50 (Dark Blue) – Used for headings and titles to give a maritime feel.
Secondary Color: #E74C3C (Crab Red) – Used for accents, links, and important buttons to bring a lively contrast.
Background Color: #f4f4f4 (Light Gray) – Used as the overall background to keep the site light and easy to read.

Typography

Font 1: Arial, sans-serif – Used for body text and paragraphs, ensuring legibility.
Font 2: Georgia, serif – Used for headings and titles to give a more traditional, nautical feel.

Wireframe

Mobile View:

  • Header with logo and menu icon for easy navigation
  • Hero section with a photo of crabbing on the Chesapeake Bay
  • Main content with top tips for crabbing and seasonal information
  • Footer with contact information and social media links

Desktop View:

  • Header with logo, navigation bar, and a search option
  • Sidebar with upcoming events, local news, and weather reports for crabbing
  • Main content section with detailed crabbing guides, equipment reviews, and interactive maps
  • Footer with a map of crabbing areas, contact details, and a forum link
