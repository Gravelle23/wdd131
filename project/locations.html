Top Crabbing Locations

Explore the best spots for crabbing in Southern Maryland, including public docks, piers, and hidden gems along the Chesapeake Bay.

1. Point Lookout State Park

Known for its scenic views and abundant blue crabs, this is a favorite spot for locals. Accessible piers make it great for families.

2. Solomon's Island Fishing Pier

Offers great crabbing in addition to fishing. You'll often find fellow crabbers sharing tips and stories here.

3. Bushwood Wharf

A quiet, lesser-known spot perfect for crabbing with minimal crowds. Bring your own gear and enjoy a peaceful crabbing experience.

4. Benedict Bridge Area

Great place to drop a trotline and spend the afternoon. Best during early morning hours or evenings.
